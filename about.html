About Anu

Who Anu Is

Anu is the passionate creator behind Zara Crochet Work, crafting beautiful handmade pieces with love and dedication. There is not a moment that you will not see her without her yarn or thread and crochet hook crocheting away. She crochets with a passion like it is part of her life.

Her Story

Anu began her crochet journey in 2021, initially creating special handmade items for her beloved daughter Zara. At first, there were simple projects for a beginner to learn like granny squares and potholders. As years have passed, a lot has changed. Now there are shawls, blankets, sweaters, ponchos, doilies, toys, and so much more that can be made. The items are endless! Not just the "good ole squares" as they used to say.

Why Choose Us

With several years of dedicated crochet experience since 2021, every piece Anu creates carries the same love and care she put into those very first items for her daughter Zara. She carefully selects only high-quality yarns and threads, ensuring each handcrafted piece will be treasured for years to come.
